PAS catalogue

This track displays polyadenylation sites (PAS) obtained with SCINPAS workflow from 55 scRNA-seq N2 and VC2010 worm embryonic cell samples from Packer, Jonathan S., et al. A lineage-resolved molecular atlas of C. elegans embryogenesis at single-cell resolution. Science 65.6459 2019).

Full PAS catalogue is separated into substracks based on genomic strand.

Stringency level, the maximal motif percentage at which the PAS is retained upon filtration, is documented in the "score" value of the PAS, has range (0-100), and visulized by color: the darker the color, the higher the score.

For example, PAS score=70 means that the PAS is retained when one sorts PAS catalogue by PAS usage level and requires 70% of PAS to have canonical polyA signal presence in the upstream vicinity.

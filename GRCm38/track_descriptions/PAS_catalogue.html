PAS catalogue¶

This track displays polyadenylation sites (PAS) obtained with SCINPAS workflow from 188 scRNA-seq samples from Tabula Muris Senis database.

Full PAS catalogue is separated into substracks based on genomic strand.

Stringency level, the maximal motif percentage at which the PAS is retained upon filtration, is documented in the "score" value of the PAS, has range (0-100), and visulized by color: the darker the color, the higher the score.

For example, PAS score=57 means that the PAS is retained when one sorts PAS catalogue by PAS usage level and requires 57% of PAS to have canonical polyA signal presence in the upstream vicinity.

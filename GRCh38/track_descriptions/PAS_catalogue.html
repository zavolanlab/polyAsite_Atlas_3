PAS catalogue¶

This track displays polyadenylation sites (PAS) obtained with SCINPAS workflow from 813 scRNA-seq samples from Human Cell Atlas and NeMO databases.

Full PAS catalogue is separated into substracks based on strand and stringency level, the maximal motif percentage at which the PAS is retained upon filtration.

Stringency level is documented in the "score" value of the PAS, has range (0-100), and visulized by color: the darker the color, the higher the score.

For example, PAS score=69 means that the PAS is retained when one sorts PAS catalogue by PAS usage level and requires 69% of PAS to have canonical polyA signal presence in the upstream vicinity.

As such, subtrack "30-100% motif presence, + strand" contains PAS on the positive genomic strand, and having score 30-100.
